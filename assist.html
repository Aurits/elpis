How We Can Help

Through our partnership program, we provide financial assistance for medical emergencies and other urgent needs. Our support network is designed to help young people in crisis situations.

Eligibility

  • Ages 15-40
  • Located in our service regions
  • Genuine medical or emergency need
  • Preference given to Elpis Partners
