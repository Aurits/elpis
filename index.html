Our Programs

Innovative approaches to youth empowerment through creative expression and community engagement.

Creative Advocacy

Training youth advocates using creative mediums to challenge misconceptions and create meaningful dialogue. Our award-winning short films and viral social media campaigns transform personal stories into powerful tools for change.

Learn More

Community Outreach

Reaching communities across six regions with culturally-sensitive health education programs. From urban Kampala to pastoral Kotido, we adapt our approach to meet each community's unique needs and cultural context.

Learn More

Strategic Partnerships

Building alliances with schools, corporations, and government entities to amplify our impact. Our collaborative approach ensures sustainable change through shared resources and expertise.
