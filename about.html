Our Journey

2022 - The Beginning

Founded by passionate advocates Nkuutu Brian Moses and Feni Desmond, driven by a vision to empower Ugandan youth through innovative approaches to health education and advocacy.

Rapid Evolution

What started as a local initiative quickly evolved into a nationwide movement, as our arts-based approach resonated with communities across Uganda.

Geographic Expansion

Established strategic presence across six key locations: Kampala, Gulu, Kotido, Luuka, Arua, and Mbarara, each with tailored programs addressing local needs.

Recognition & Validation

Our youth-centered approaches gained recognition from communities, partners, and stakeholders, validating our belief that young people are the most effective agents of change.
