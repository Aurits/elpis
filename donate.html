About the Campaign

We believe in the power of collective action to drive positive change in our communities.

In response to the pressing need for increased charitable giving in Uganda, Elpis Initiative Uganda launched the "10 Million Hearts, One Cause" campaign. This campaign under the Elpis Initiative Uganda partnership program aims to create a new and more effective way to promote giving for charity in Uganda, seeking to target 10 million individuals especially youth to contribute towards this cause.

1

HIV/AIDS and Mental Health Awareness

Elpis Initiative Uganda has a track record of using art, music, dance, drama, film and poetry to raise awareness about HIV and mental health issues. This fund enables us to expand our reach and impact, engage people with their own experiences, and help them thrive.

2

Supporting Similar Organizations

We recognize that other organizations are working tirelessly to address HIV and/or mental health issues in Uganda, but often lack the resources to do so effectively. This fund will provide financial support to such organizations, empowering them to continue carrying out their vital role.

3

Medical Emergency Assistance

For young people aged between 15-40, better medical services are necessary. This fund will provide critical financial support for hospitalization and other medical payments, ensuring that no one has to face these challenges alone.
